# Foam & Form Elementor Layout Guide

## Recommended plugin stack

- WooCommerce
- Elementor
- Astra
- Elementor Header & Footer Builder or Astra Pro later if needed
- WP Mail SMTP
- Rank Math SEO or Yoast SEO
- WooCommerce Stripe Gateway
- WooCommerce PayPal Payments
- Customer Reviews for WooCommerce
- FunnelKit Cart or Side Cart WooCommerce
- Klaviyo
- Fluent Forms or WPForms

## Homepage structure

1. Hero
- Two-column section
- Left: eyebrow, H1, supporting paragraph, two CTA buttons
- Right: lifestyle image with floating best-seller card

2. Category entry cards
- Four icon cards
- Furniture
- Foam Products
- Free Shipping $50+
- Healthy Materials

3. Best sellers
- Elementor heading
- WooCommerce Products widget filtered to featured or best-seller products

4. Brand values
- Four-to-six icon blocks
- Premium design
- Comfort engineered
- US practical shipping
- Healthy foam materials

5. Reviews
- Three testimonial cards
- Add star icons above each review

6. Lifestyle gallery
- Four square or portrait image cards
- Use real room photography, not cutout-only product renders

7. CTA section
- Strong heading
- One primary button to `/shop`

8. Recommendation block
- Insert shortcode: `[foam_form_ai_recommendations]`

## Shop page structure

1. Intro strip
- Use shortcode `[foam_form_shop_intro]`

2. Filters
- WooCommerce price filter widget
- Product categories widget
- Filter by rating widget

3. Product grid
- 4 columns desktop
- 2 columns tablet
- 1-2 columns mobile
- Enable AJAX add to cart in WooCommerce settings

## Single product page structure

1. Gallery
- Large primary image
- 4-6 supporting images

2. Summary
- Best seller badge
- Product title
- Star rating
- Price + free-shipping hint
- Bullet value props
- Materials snapshot
- Trust badge card
- Sticky add to cart mobile bar

3. Story section
- Lifestyle usage imagery
- Room-use scenarios

4. FAQ
- Keep 3-5 concise questions

5. Shipping & Returns
- Explain free shipping over $50
- Standard shipping $9.99
- 30-day returns

6. Frequently Bought Together
- Keep 3 complementary products

## About page blocks

1. Brand story
- Comfort + affordability + modern US lifestyle

2. Materials and factory
- Foam density logic
- Upholstery durability
- Packaging and assembly philosophy

3. Health and sustainability
- Low-odor materials
- Long lifecycle use
- Repair/reupholster potential

## Contact page blocks

1. Intro copy
2. Contact form
3. Support hours
4. FAQ link
5. Returns/shipping mini-help

## SEO structure

- Homepage title: `Modern Furniture & Foam Products | Foam & Form`
- Shop title: `Shop Sofa Beds, Memory Foam & Cushions | Foam & Form`
- Product titles should include core keyword + use case
- Write unique meta descriptions for top products
- Use Product schema, Review schema, FAQ schema where possible
- Compress all imagery before upload

## Email and conversion additions

- Klaviyo signup block in footer and exit-intent popup later
- Abandoned cart via Klaviyo or FunnelKit Automations
- Post-purchase review flow after 10 days
- Welcome email with best-seller collection links
